Project Experience

We have operated in companies with entirely different products and applications. Each case presented a new challenge for which we successfully provided a solution and, at the same time, reduced costs and increased profitability

Here are some examples:

Test Rig Upgrade For 5G Fixed Wireless Nodes

A large purchase request for a prior product meant the client needed to recommission their production test rig, however, after assembly the test rig did not function as expected. I studied their in-house program to discover that a firmware update of a test instrument was incompatible with their custom control module.

We rewrote all their instrument control modules to make them more robust and be able to handle atypical events. We also redesigned the method for controlling a positioning motor which waived the need for human interaction during the test. This allowed them to test units on the production line which otherwise they were not able to do.

Wi-Fi And Bluetooth Testing

The client gave an extremely tight deadline to achieve their targets and meet the schedule of test certification. Previously, more than 150k data points from their tests results were manually organised and processed; taking an unreasonable period and unnecessarily occupying the time of the technical personnel.

The conclusion was a concept created to not only automate data collection but process the result and present these in a report which would also clearly show anomalies, errors, missing data and data statistics; the data report is available in less than a minute, equally, any revisions or updates are concluded almost instantly – saving months of engineering time for the client’s core technology.

Other Key Developments

The automated data collection method also showed there were discrepancies due to the testing facilities and, as an example, I introduced a Wi-Fi test which demonstrated the deficiencies of the RF loss due to wiring.

RF Performance For Magnetic Induction Communication

A new revision of this client’s chip design was being tested before entering production. Following restructuring within the organisation, it became necessary to change the test procedures to operate with the new revision.

After adapting the tests to the hardware changes, it was revealed that the latest chip under performed the predecessor. We implemented further testing to investigate relevant tuning parameters and we was able to discover values that optimised the performance above the previous design.

DVB Demodulation Performance Of System On A Chip

In this case, the Algorithm Design Engineers were improving the performance of DVB demodulation on their product platform in order to outperform the competition. They were in need of additional testing capabilities for the new standard and to significantly reduce the duration of the tests.

We implemented the test for the DVB-S2X standard, modernised the test search algorithms and control methods with a threefold increase in productivity. Additionally, we was able to carry out test procedures in parallel by calibrating broadcasting equipment and increasing testing output, which effectively saved investments that would otherwise have proved impossible to provide.
