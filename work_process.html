Strategy for Success

Designing and developing your product is dynamic and we believe it is essential to use a workflow that complements that nature.

Having experienced many project environments, we have developed a process that allows for variability whist maintaining sight of the core objective.

Process High-Level Overview

Test Design and Development

For the results to have meaning we need to know the purpose of the measurement, and the confidence of the measurement is based on verifiability; we need a record of how they were measured. A requirements document describes what the measurements are intended to demonstrate and how to execute them. This creates a clear set of criteria of how to build the tests.

Acquisition and Interpretation

As the engineers evaluate the data collected from the the product they will make discoveries of how their product performs and these will lead to new requirements to explore their product in more detail. This gives them the essential feedback that helps them understand and improve their design.
